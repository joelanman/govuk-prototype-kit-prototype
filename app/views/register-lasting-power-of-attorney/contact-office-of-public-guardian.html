Contact the Office of the Public Guardian (OPG)

Contact the Office of the Public Guardian if you need help with making and registering an LPA.

If you email or write to OPG include:

  • your full name, address and date of birth
  • whether you are the attorney or the donor
  • the donor’s full name, address and date of birth (if you’re the attorney)
  • the LPA reference number (if it’s about an existing LPA)

If you have a concern

If you have concerns about either the LPA or the donor’s wellbeing, contact OPG’s Safeguarding Unit as soon as possible.
